FAQ

Ordering questions

Is this a card checkout?

No. There is no Stripe and no card processing. This MVP creates an order and shows Crypto or PayPal instructions.

How does bulk pricing work?

If quantity reaches the listed bulk minimum, the cart applies the bulk unit price. If the list marks bulk as unavailable, the cart shows bulk quote required.

What does SUMMER do?

SUMMER is auto-filled and locked for now. It applies exactly 5% off priced cart items.

How is shipping handled?

Shipping is confirmed by the seller after the address and order details are reviewed.
