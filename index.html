Shipping process

From request to reviewed shipment

  1. Submit request. Add products, quantities, destination, buyer type, and document needs.
  2. Sales review. WWB reviews availability, product class, buyer context, and destination requirements.
  3. Documentation. COA, batch docs, MSDS, invoice, or export docs are confirmed where available.
  4. Quote confirmation. A confirmed quote and next steps are provided outside the public catalog.

FAQ

Common sales-desk questions

Can I purchase directly from the catalog?

No. The public catalog is a quote-request intake tool. Sales review is required before any order can proceed.

Why are prices not public?

Availability, account terms, product configuration, destination rules, and documentation requirements can affect quote terms.

Which documents can be requested?

COA, batch records, MSDS, invoice, and export documents can be requested and confirmed during review.

Do restricted products require verification?

Yes. Product class and destination may require licenses, prescriptions, business credentials, import approval, or institutional verification.

Policies

Compliance, privacy, and quote terms

Compliance notice

Regulated products require proper licensing, prescriptions, import approval, or institutional/business verification where applicable.

Restricted products policy

Injectables, hormones, prescription items, raw materials, and export-sensitive products are not available through unrestricted checkout.

Privacy policy

Terms of use

Catalog content is for B2B inquiry and sales review. It is not medical advice, dosing guidance, or a public pharmacy service.

Refund / cancellation / quote policy

Quotes are not orders. Payment, cancellation, replacement, or refund terms must be confirmed by sales desk before fulfillment.

Documentation policy
